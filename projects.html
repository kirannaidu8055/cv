Cleartrip (Bangalore : Offsite)

Apr 2015 to Present:

Project Name: Cleartrip Activities, Cleartrip Activity Management Portal (CAMP) Activities project aims at providing a hand-picked and comprehensive collection of activities, experiences, and things to do in a particular city city (Ex: Online ticketing system for such as culinary classes, food walks, segway tours, luxury bike rentals, yachting, adventure, Go karting, photography trails, hiking, kids, day outings, night treks etc). It comprises of two parts:

  • Suppliers Management Portal called CAMP.
  • Consumer front (Mainly Android, iOS and Windows App).
CAMP backend is completely built on RoR (Ruby on Rails) which serves as the engine for the supplier portal. Supplier portal mainly involves registration of new service providers, supplier activity creation, curation of activity and final publish.Consumer App uses the search API calls based city and individual activity and displays it to the mobile app users, other modules include booking.
